If my marks are less than the previous marks in any subject after repeating, will my marks decrease?

No. Marks are never decreased when you improve any subject in Board. For example, if you already got 170 marks (1st Year 90 + 2nd Year 80) in Chemistry and you repeat it. After repeating, you get 160 marks (1st Year 75 + 2nd Year 85). Here, your old marks (170) will be shown in the final result card. A student is never at loss while improving any subject.

If I repeat complete 1st year or complete 2nd year, do I have to give my practicals again?

No. Practicals are re-conducted only if you repeat a complete subject (1st Year + 2nd Year).

I want to improve my Intermediate marks. Can I repeat only Physics and Chemistry of 1st Year?

You cannot repeat the subjects of 1st year only. If you choose to improve any subject, you’ll have to take the examination of 1st Year and 2nd Year both. According to Punjab Board Policy, You can repeat: Complete Intermediate Complete 1st Year Complete 2nd Year Any Subject(s) of 1st Year + 2nd Year
